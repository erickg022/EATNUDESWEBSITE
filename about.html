Ryan and Tim — Co-Founders

A dream come true.

Two founders with one clear idea: to build a food brand that made healthy eating taste genuinely good — with real ingredients, honest portions, and no guilt attached.

Ryan and Tim started with a simple frustration: healthy food was either bland, overcomplicated, or packed with ingredients nobody could pronounce. They believed better food didn't have to feel like a compromise. It could be fresh, flavorful, filling, and made from real ingredients people actually understood.

So they set out to create a brand that could change the way people thought about healthy eating. Not as a trend. Not as a diet. But as everyday food that tasted good, felt good, and fit naturally into people's lives.
